Who we are?

We are a meal prep service that focuses on providing a healthy menu to individuals with developmental disabilities in the Phoenix area. We deliver to day programs and employment centers.

Why do we do this?

After years of experience working in the field, we identified a nutritional need which led us to take initiative by providing healthy and tasty meals. Our goal is to create a social impact by contributing to the health and wellbeing of this community.
